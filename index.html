Currently

I work as a junior software engineer at IKEA, contributing to creating a better everyday life for the many people, which is what motivates and excites me most about being part of this great company.

Education

I hold a degree in Journalism, specializing in Integrated Communication. Upon graduation, I received the First Job Program Award from the Madrid Press Association. I completed a Master’s in Investigative Reporting. Additionally, I have a degree in Criminology.

Later, I attended the intensive full-stack web development bootcamp at Ironhack, followed by web programming courses at Adalab and internal training at the consulting firm KairosDS, where I was selected among nearly 200 candidates to work with them. Later, I also completed an internal GCP-focused bootcamp at IKEA.

I love learning languages. I learned English and Italian abroad as a child. A few years ago, I started learning German.

Professional Experience

My career began in journalism, working mostly as an intern for almost a decade in national media such as Telecinco, El Mundo, Cuatro, RTVE, and Antena 3, developing skills in research, communication, and storytelling.

I translated the book Dateline Jerusalem (by Chris Mitchell, historian and chief correspondent for CBN News in the Middle East). Years later, I ventured into the import sector, running a small company specializing in importing Mexican spirits brands, a very early-stage project that could not continue due to the COVID pandemic.

Afterwards, I worked as a frontend developer for the Austrian software company In-Manas, in a fully remote position. Later, I worked at KairósDS, and I am currently working at IKEA.

Skills and Working Style

I am a proactive, well-organized, pragmatic, and adaptable professional, with strong communication skills and attention to detail. I enjoy working in collaborative teams, where transparency, respect, trust, and mutual support allow us to achieve high-quality results.

I like to study and constantly learn from everything and everyone. I always aim to understand the full context of problems before tackling them.

I deeply appreciate and value all feedback I receive. Fail fast, fail early, fail often.
